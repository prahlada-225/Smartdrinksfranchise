Mobile Smoothie Unit Investment

With this franchise option, you can offer customers all- natural real fruit smoothies and gourmet coffees in convenient and repeatable high-traffic locations. Our mobile smoothie vans are designed to capture the attention of passersby and create a memorable experience. We have carefully selected these locations based on their potential to attract a large customer base.

By operating in these predetermined areas, franchisees can maximize their earning potential and establish a loyal customer following. With our focus on using only the freshest and most natural ingredients, franchisees can confidently serve high- quality products that appeal to health-conscious consumers. Join us in the mobile smoothie van franchise and become part of the Smart Drinks & Nutrition® success story.

Minimal Staffing Requirement

At Smart Drinks & Nutrition® , we have carefully optimized the operations of our mobile units to ensure maximum efficiency and cost-effectiveness. We recommend a staffing model where no more than two individuals are required to run the mobile unit successfully. By keeping the staffing requirements to a minimum, we help our franchisees reduce their labor costs without compromising the quality of service or the overall customer experience. This streamlined approach allows franchisees to focus their resources on delivering exceptional Smart Drinks and providing personalized attention to their valued customers. With our efficient staffing model, franchisees can maximize productivity, maintain low overhead costs, and ultimately increase profitability. At Smart Drinks & Nutrition® , we are committed to supporting our franchisees in achieving operational excellence and financial success by offering smart and efficient solutions for their staffing needs.
