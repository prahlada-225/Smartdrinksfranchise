Royalty 6% / Advertising Fee 3% of Gross Sales

At Smart Drinks & Nutrition® , we believe in transparency when it comes to the fees associated with our franchise opportunity. Our Royalty Fee, set at 6% of gross sales, ensures that our franchisees have ongoing support and access to our established brand. In addition, we have monthly fees dedicated to Advertising, with 3% of gross revenues allocated towards national marketing initiatives. We also emphasize local advertising, with a minimum of $900 or 2% of Gross Revenues (whichever is greater) allocated towards promoting your specific location. Furthermore, we have a Technology Fee of $150, which covers the costs of maintaining and updating our advanced technology systems that support your business operations. We provide detailed explanations of all these fees in our comprehensive Franchise Disclosure Document, ensuring that you have a clear understanding of the investment required to join our Smart Drinks & Nutrition® family.
